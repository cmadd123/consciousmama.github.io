Questions? We've got answers.

Is MomRise free? +
MomRise offers a 7-day free trial so you can experience everything before committing. After your trial, it's $6.99/month — cancel anytime from your account settings. There are no hidden fees and no annual contract required.
How are learning paths personalized? +
When you describe a challenge you're facing, MomRise creates a personalized learning path based on your child's age and developmental stage. Each path includes step-by-step lessons, parent tips, and guidance for when things don't go as planned.
Can I track multiple children? +
Absolutely! You can add as many children as you need. Each child gets their own profile with personalized milestones and learning paths based on their individual age and needs.
Is my data private and secure? +
Your privacy is our priority. We never sell your personal information. All data is stored securely, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
What ages is this app designed for? +
MomRise is designed for children from birth through elementary school (ages 0-8). The app automatically adjusts learning paths and milestones based on your child's age.
