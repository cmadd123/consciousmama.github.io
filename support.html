Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a child to my account?

Tap the "My Kids" section from the home screen, then tap the "+" button to add a new child. Enter their name and birthdate to personalize their experience.

How do AI Learning Paths work?

Learning Paths are personalized activity plans generated by AI based on your child's age and the specific challenges you're facing. Simply describe what you'd like help with, and we'll create a step-by-step guide with parent tips.

Can I track multiple children?

Yes! You can add multiple children to your account and switch between their profiles to track milestones and activities separately for each child.

What's the difference between Standard and Comfort mode?

Standard mode features a bright, colorful interface. Comfort mode offers a calmer, darker theme that's easier on the eyes, especially during evening use.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@consciousmama.app. We'll process your request within 48 hours.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is stored securely using Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. We never sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
